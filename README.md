### Selected projects

#### [PianoPro](https://github.com/Quentin-Piot/piano-pro)
**Rust · GPU · MIDI · Music Learning**

A GPU-accelerated piano learning and MIDI visualization app built on top of [Neothesia](https://github.com/PolyMeilex/Neothesia).  
Adds per-track controls and a transport bar. Sheet music display, auto-transcription, and structured practice tools are the next goals.

#### [prediction-market-backtester](https://github.com/Quentin-Piot/prediction-market-backtester)
**Python · Backtesting · Quant Research**

A correctness-first backtesting engine for prediction markets such as Polymarket and Kalshi.  
Designed around explicit execution assumptions, reproducible runs, and strategy experimentation across momentum, mean-reversion, and event-threshold setups.
